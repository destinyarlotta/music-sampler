Bruno Mars is a best-selling artist with over 130 million records sold worldwide. Born Peter Gene Hernandez, he is an American singer-songwriter with the stage name of Bruno Mars. His soft, sensitive voice exudes emotion and expression. Bruno Mars sings as if he has experienced both pain and wonder, and therefore is relatable to any listener globally. His songs cover a variety of universal themes including: love, relationships, friendship, happiness, wealth, and success. He performs each show as if it were his last, with infectious passion and energy that is irressistably toe-tapping.
